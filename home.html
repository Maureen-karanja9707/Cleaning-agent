Who We Are

Expert Cleaning & Fumigation

Transparent Cleaning Company is a professional cleaning and fumigation company dedicated to delivering top-tier hygiene solutions for both residential and commercial spaces. We specialize in domestic cleaning services, including sofa set, carpet, mattress, house, and post-construction cleaning, as well as toilet stain and odor removal.

Our fumigation and pest control services effectively eliminate household pests such as bedbugs, cockroaches, mosquitoes, mice, and bees, ensuring a safe and comfortable living environment. We also provide commercial fumigation services for offices, warehouses, food-grade premises, and hospitals, maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and sanitation.

At Transparent Cleaning Company, we prioritize customer satisfaction by using eco-friendly products, advanced cleaning techniques, and a professional team committed to excellence. Whether you need routine cleaning or intensive pest control, we guarantee reliable and efficient services tailored to your needs.
